Imagine getting such blog-style letters from all your loved ones when they have an update on their lives to share
with you. Imagine yourself typing up one letter to your loved ones to bring them up to speed on your life.
All you have to do is sit down for five minutes and write up a short life update. Won't you feel intimately
connected with them if you make this a practice that does not involve much time commitment on your part?
There is no noise. No scanning through hundreds of posts. You are guaranteed to find things that matter to
your inner circles and that matter to you to enable you to support them, motivate them, assist them,
congratulate them and just remained informed of their personal developments.
You can broadcast a life update to all the people that matter to you in few minutes by just typing it once. Instead of wasting time in engaging in on and off, superficial conversations, through The Pigeon, you can engage with a group of selected people in genuine and simple conversations that truly fills them in on your life, milestones, goals, passion and desires. At The Pigeon, we aspire to bring back the practice of sending traditional letters. That being said, in order to keep up with the fast-paced world, we do not intend to say good riddance to digital messaging. We have set out to use technology to get the intimate experience of receiving letters at regular intervals back into our lives. With The Pigeon, you can now fill in all the people that matter to you on your life by merely filling out a small form. Our product be used by anyone and everyone from all walks of life. It is simple and intuitive. It reintroduces intimacy in our interactions. It will be a hybrid of social media, blogs and letters. We take the personal touch and simplicity of letters, writing style from blogs and updates from social media. Here is what our product does in a very simple, fast and convenient fashion: You can update people in your inner circles within a couple minutes (unlike writing letters or making calls) You can make sure your personal update does not get lost (unlike in Facebook and Twitter) You are guaranteed to get only personal updates from your inner circles when you use our product. No advertisements, noise or information overload.
